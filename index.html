About

I'm Lina – an aspiring UX/UI-Designer & immersive media developer based in Berlin!

I’m currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Media Informatics at the Berlin University of Applied Sciences (BHT). Previously, I earned my Bachelor’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction, where I developed a strong foundation in user-centered design. Alongside my studies, I work at Fraunhofer IPK as a UX designer and Unity developer, crafting intuitive interfaces and immersive experiences for research-driven projects.

When I'm not designing or coding, you’ll probably find me bouldering, trying out new coffee spots, or getting inspired by Berlin’s creative corners :)
